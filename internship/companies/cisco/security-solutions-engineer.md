# Security Solutions Engineer, Cisco

> 08-16-2021

## What You’ll Do

- Define and assist in small-scale projects to improve operational efforts
- Understand common application, network vulnerabilities and techniques for testing and exploitation
- Practice and emulate real world attack tactics in simulated lab environments
- Become familiar with a number of security testing tools, including Kali Linux, Nmap, Metasploit and BurpSuite
- Help clients assess their level of risk in using technology within the business and provide recommendations to reduce business risks
- Work with clients to strategize on effective controls around people and technology to prevent unnecessary risk while meeting compliance requirements
- Shadow a variety of client engagements including risk assessments, penetration tests, and IR investigations

## Implementation Services

Implementation Services Consultants with Cisco’s Security Services team bring deep expertise on Cisco security products to our customers. Our team assists customers with developing strategies for deploying new technologies in their environments, assist in designing and implementing new technologies, handle the integration of Cisco technology into existing security portfolios and assist customers in optimizing existing and new security technologies. Our customers expect Cisco to be authorities in security technology…the Integration team are those guides.

## What You’ll Do

- Attend on-site design and configuration sessions with a Cisco Security Solutions mentor
- Identify and support in large-scale-design efforts for customer network integrations
- Support in gathering requirements from customers for projects to meet critical business goals
- Work with a security team mentor to define the business goals and requirements for integration efforts
- Configure lab devices with configuration templates or system configurations which will be used in customers’ production implementations
- Aid in analyzing and designing policy creation for Identity Services, Firepower Intrusion Detection, ASA Firewall Rules, and Catalyst Switch Configurations
- Analyze customer configurations and provide feedback on possible improvements or changes to the configuration to improve alignment with customer business goals
- Analyze customer logs or net flow data to advise on improvements aligned with their business goals

## Who You Are

- You're currently pursuing a bachelor’s or master’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or Information Systems (or equivalent) with software development skill set or interest in information security
- You have knowledge of IT systems, networking concepts, TCP/IP protocols, network devices and applications, OSI 7-layer model, or code development
- You have a desire and passion for information security and willingness to learn and teach yourself tough concepts
- You are able to work well in small dynamic teams and balance multiple responsibilities
- You have the ability to work in collaborative teams distributed across the US
- You possess excellent written and verbal skills
